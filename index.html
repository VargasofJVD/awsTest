About CITSA UCC

Our Vision

To be the leading tech community that shapes the next generation of technology leaders in Africa through innovation, collaboration, and excellence.

Our Mission

To foster innovation and professional growth among IT students through practical learning experiences, industry connections, and collaborative projects.

Our Values

Excellence, Innovation, Collaboration, Integrity, and Continuous Learning form the foundation of our community's principles.
